The Best Movies According to Natalie

My top 3 movies in the last 5 years.

A Man Called Otto

This movie reminded me that we should never give up on people. Everybody is struggling in their own way. This is the movie of the year!

True Spirit

Based on a true story. This will let you get excited and lead you to tears. This girl's endurance is amazing!

Instant Family

Everybody is looking for that feeling of belonging somewhere. Based on a true story, this comedy will let you root for this family.
